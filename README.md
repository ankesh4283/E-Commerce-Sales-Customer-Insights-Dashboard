
# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

## 📌 End-to-End Data Analytics Project (SQL + Power BI)

A comprehensive business intelligence project focused on analyzing **sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency** in an e-commerce environment.

This solution transforms raw transactional data into **actionable insights** that help stakeholders improve **customer retention, delivery performance, and revenue growth**.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

E-commerce businesses often lack visibility into:

* Customer retention and repeat purchase behavior
* Revenue concentration across regions and categories
* Delivery performance and its impact on satisfaction
* Key drivers behind customer reviews and engagement

This project addresses these gaps with a **data-driven dashboard for decision-making**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To design an interactive dashboard that enables:

* Monitoring of **revenue, orders, and customer metrics**
* Identification of **repeat vs one-time customers**
* Analysis of **customer segmentation (High / Medium / Low value)**
* Evaluation of **delivery efficiency and delays**
* Breakdown of **sales by category and region**
* Understanding of **customer satisfaction trends**

---

## 📊 Executive Summary

* 💰 **$16.01M Total Revenue**
* 📦 **99K+ Orders Processed**
* 👥 **96K+ Customers**
* 🔁 **~3K Repeat Customers (~3%)**
* 💳 **$166 Avg Revenue per Customer**

* ⭐ **4.1 Avg Customer Rating**

> 📌 **Core Insight:**
> Revenue performance is strong, but **customer retention is critically low**, indicating dependency on continuous new customer acquisition.

## 📌 Key Business Insights

### 👥 Customer Retention is Critically Low

* Only ~3% of customers are repeat buyers
* Majority (~93K) are one-time customers

**Insight:**
The business is heavily dependent on **new customer acquisition**, which is costly and unsustainable long-term.
➡️ Indicates a weak retention strategy and low customer lifetime value (CLV)

---

### 💰 Revenue is Strong but Not Loyalty-Driven

* High total revenue ($16M+) despite low repeat customers

**Insight:**
Revenue growth is driven by **new users, not loyalty**
➡️ Risk: Growth may decline if acquisition slows

---

### 🌍 Revenue is Highly Concentrated in Few States

* São Paulo (SP) contributes the highest revenue
* Other states contribute significantly less

**Insight:**
Business is **geographically dependent**
➡️ Opportunity to expand in underperforming regions

---

### 🛍 Sales are Dominated by Top Categories

* Health & Beauty, Watches, Bed & Bath lead revenue

**Insight:**
Revenue follows the **Pareto principle (80/20 rule)**
➡️ Few categories generate most of the revenue

---

### 🚚 Delivery Performance is Strong but Inconsistent

* 93%+ orders delivered on time
* Late delivery spikes in certain months

**Insight:**
Operations are efficient overall but **inconsistent during peak periods**
➡️ Impacts customer experience and satisfaction

---

### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction is Good but Not Exceptional

* Average rating: 4.1 / 5

**Insight:**
Customers are satisfied but not highly delighted
➡️ Opportunity to improve experience and service quality

---

### 👑 High-Value Customers Drive Revenue

* Small segment contributes a large share of revenue

**Insight:**
Business relies on **high-value customers**
➡️ Retention of these customers is critical

---

### 💳 Payment Method Preference

* Credit card dominates transactions

**Insight:**
Customers prefer **convenient payment methods**
➡️ Opportunity to optimize checkout and offers

---

## 💼 Strategic Recommendations

* 🎯 Improve **customer retention** (loyalty programs, personalization)
* 📦 Optimize **delivery operations during peak periods**
* 🌍 Expand in **low-performing regions**
* 🛍 Invest in **top-performing categories**
* ⭐ Enhance **customer experience**
* 💡 Focus on **high-value customer retention strategies**
